MISSION

Our mission is to inspire students to design and build robots capable of completing diverse tasks across three exciting and competitive rounds. This competition fosters critical thinking, teamwork, and engineering innovation.

ROBOT REQUIREMENTS

  • Maximum Robot Dimensions: 30 x 30 cm
  • Battery Requirement: One battery per robot
  • Control Type: Manual or wireless (depending on the task)
  • Allowed Microcontrollers: Arduino / ESP (Arduino Nano preferred)
  • Number of Robots: One robot per team

ROUND 1 ‚Äì Balloon Popping

Description: The robot must pop balloons that are identified as "correct" while avoiding those marked as "wrong." Balloons will be placed randomly in the arena.

Scoring:

  • Correct Balloon: +3 points
  • Wrong Balloon: ‚àí2 points

Challenge: Precision and timing are essential.

ROUND 2 ‚Äì LEGO Shape Builder

Description: Teams will use LEGO pieces to build a specific shape provided by the organizers.

Evaluation Criteria:

  • Shape accuracy
  • Time efficiency
  • Creativity

ROUND 3 ‚Äì Maze by Line Follower

Description: The robot must navigate a maze autonomously by following a defined path (usually a black or white line).

Scoring: Based on total time taken and the number of path errors or deviations.

GENERAL CONDITIONS

  • Ramps: A slope will be present between each round to test the robot‚Äôs mobility and balance.
  • Final Score: Teams will accumulate points from all three rounds.
  • Penalties: Points may be deducted for violations such as intentional collisions, off-task behavior, or inactivity.

TEAM REQUIREMENTS

  • Team Size: 2 to 6 students
  • Eligibility: Participants must be 16+ years old (open to high school and university students)
